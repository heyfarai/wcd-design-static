Guidione Machava

Co-Founder & Product Design Lead 

Guidione Machava is a product designer with 10+ years of experience turning complex ideas into usable, impactful tools.

From MVPs to enterprise platforms, hes designed for startups, global orgs, and high-growth teamsincluding blockchain products at Shopify and digital systems for emerging markets at the World Bank.

Hes also played product manager, leading cross-functional teams across three continents.

Founder of World-Class Designer School and author of Design Sutra, Guidione brings systems thinking, sharp execution, and a global perspective to every build.

Farai Madzima

Co-Founder & Head of UX Strategy

Farai Madzima is a UX strategist with 15+ years of experience designing enterprise software that scales and simplifies.

At Shopify, he helped launch Shopify Balance Mobile and led UX for Shopify Capital ($5B+ in SMB loans). Before that, he built and led Standard Banks UX team across 18 African markets.

Hes the founder of Pixel Up! and a sought-after speaker at global events like IXDA and Leading Design.

Farai brings systems thinking, inclusive strategy, and practical leadership to teams building complex, high-impact products.
